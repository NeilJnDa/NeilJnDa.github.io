Once Upon A Time There Was A Black Bird

Best Team Award and Best Game Nominate

Game for good game Jam 2022: itch page

Escape-room style puzzles, Turn-Based Card Game

Explore the chessboard-like dark room and use the cards you collect to escape the blackbirds. The room layout corresponds horribly to the chessboard puzzles, where manic depression and loneliness need to be faced head-on, and you are accompanied by Bunnypropion​ and Deer Burns, as well as a forgotten but so important one: a friend in old time.

Among the stars

2022 CiGA Game Jam: itch page

Existence is not necessarily reasonable, and the deceit around us makes us alienate ourselves. Is this the only thing that we can do, an infinite loop that no one can escape? Daidai Mouse wanted to see more but is afraid to see more. 

In “Among the dreams”, the motivation for exploration and the feeling of playing is deeply combined. Even with an excellent soundtrack and a unique art style, this “anti-play” trickery will reveal itself after the playthrough.

Shadow Trace

NetEast Minigame Competation Final, Developed in 72 hours.

A two-line point-and-click puzzle game.

The theme “shadow” is reflected in a strong correspondence between reality and the past in the game. The whole scene is divided into upper and lower areas.

The game unfolds in this way, and the answers to the puzzles are presented in this up-and-down mutual influence.
