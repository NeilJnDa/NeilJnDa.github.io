Planets

GN! Online Jam: Itch.io Page

The distance can be extended to an astronomical unit, and the planet becomes small even if it carries all of our memories. Flying over small planets is a meditation about where to come and where to go.

Shadow Trace

NetEast Minigame Competation Final, Developed in 72 hours.

A two-line point-and-click puzzle game.

The theme “shadow” is reflected in a strong correspondence between reality and the past in the game. The whole scene is divided into upper and lower areas.

The game unfolds in this way, and the answers to the puzzles are presented in this up-and-down mutual influence.
