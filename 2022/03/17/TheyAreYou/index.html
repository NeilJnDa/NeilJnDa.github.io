They Are You

Won the Award of the Best Student Game of Finland, Second Place, Bit1 Competition 2022. Itch Page

My role: Programmer

  • Designed and developed the data-driven code structure with Unity and C#.

  • Programmed main gameplay content including character control, transformation logic, combat system, enemy vision logic and graphics, etc.

Click here to see the trailer!

They Are You is a puzzle/action game where you become who you kill. Possess your enemies and use their unique abilities to brave the dangers of the Tower. 

Plan your moves carefully, each mistake is fatal! Who will you kill first? Who will you become?

  • Special swapping game mechanic

  • Puzzle/Action genre

  • Hardcore die & retry

  • Lovecraftian babylonian setting

Bit1 Competition 2022

We are proud to win the Award of the Best Student Game of Finland, Second Place in Bit1 Competition 2022. We appreciate the chance to get our game exposed and receive valuable feedbacks.
